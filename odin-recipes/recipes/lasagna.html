Salad Lasagna

Description

This vegan lasagna was allegedly created by an engineer for a vegan friend, who was visiting. It consists of layers of iceberg lettuce, tomato, and onion. There has been great controversy over whether this dish is truly a lasagna, or whether it is just...
an impasta!

Ingredients

  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Tomatoes, sliced
  • Onions, sliced
  • Ranch

Steps

  1. Cover the bottom of a baking dish with a layer of the tomatoes
  2. Lay a layer of onions on top of the tomatoes
  3. Lay a layer of iceberg lettuce on top of the onion
  4. Lay another layer of tomato
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 until desired height is reached
  6. Serve with a healthy dollop of ranch and enjoy!
