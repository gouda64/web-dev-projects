None Pizza with Left Beef

Description

None pizza with left beef is an internet classic consisting of a pizza with nothing but beef chunks on the left side. It was originally conceived by Steve Molaro on his blog The Sneeze to test Domino's left-right orientation correctness. You can read the original blog post here.

This attractive pizza speaks to the true nature of the world by laying bare its doughy interior. The beef symbolizes the decadence than can come from simplicity, or perhaps simply the value of the balance between prosperity and humbleness. We have much to learn from this delicious work of art. Plato would be proud.

Ingredients

  • Some money

Steps

  1. Go on the Domino's website and select delivery (delivery is crucial because you're lazy)
  2. Follow their instructions until you get to the page with pizza options
  3. Select hand-tossed pizza and choose any size you desire
  4. On the toppings page, change cheese and sauce to none
  5. Select the left option for the beef topping
  6. Wait for your delectable masterpiece to arrive!
